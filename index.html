Investing Risks

Investing, especially for beginners, involves a certain level of risk. It is important to understand that market fluctuations, economic factors, and even unexpected events can impact investments. Here's what you should know:

  • Market Volatility: The value of investments can go up and down, which means that potential gains are often accompanied by risks of losses.
  • Risk of Loss: There's always a chance that your investment might lose value, which is especially common in high-risk assets such as stocks.
  • Liquidity Risk: Some investments may be hard to sell quickly without losing value, so it's important to be prepared for long-term commitments.
